Quick Start#

Note

This guide is designed to be used with the miniscope v4. To use the OEC with other devices, please refer to the more more detailed guides.

  1. Mount the OEC above the behavioral setup such that the animal can traverse the setup’s full area

    • The mounting holes are…

  2. Establish all electrical connections:

    • Connect the head-mounted device’s tether to the OEC’s bottom connector

    • Connect the acquisition system’s connector to the OEC’s top connector

    • Connect a computer to the OEC using a USB-A to micro-USB cable

    Attention

    • All cables must be appropriate length and within-spec (More info in User Manual)

    • While establishing electrical connections, ensure cables are not within range to interfere with tether

  3. Configure Bonsai

    1. Download Bonsai

    2. Install packages required for your experiment:

      • Bonsai.StarterPack

      • Bonsai.Miniscope

    3. Configure commutator port correctly under the PortName property of the Commutator node.
